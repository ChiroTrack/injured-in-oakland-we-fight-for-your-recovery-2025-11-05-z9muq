About Pre Chiropractic

Founded in 2015, Pre Chiropractic was born from a simple mission: to revolutionize how injured individuals recover after accidents. Our founder, Dr. Patricia Chen, witnessed too many patients struggle through fragmented care systems where legal representation and medical treatment operated independently. She envisioned a better way - a unified approach where expert attorneys, rehabilitation specialists, and healthcare providers work seamlessly together. Today, Pre Chiropractic has helped thousands of Oakland residents recover fully and receive the compensation they deserve, establishing ourselves as the region's most trusted integrated injury recovery center.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

Pre Chiropractic operates on a completely free consultation basis. We accept Personal Injury Protection (PIP) insurance directly, so you pay nothing out of pocket for your rehabilitation services. Our legal partners work on a contingency basis, meaning they only get paid when you receive your settlement. There are absolutely no upfront costs, hidden fees, or financial obligations. We believe everyone deserves access to quality legal representation and rehabilitation regardless of their financial situation.

Recovery timelines vary significantly depending on the severity of your injuries, your age, overall health, and how well you follow your rehabilitation program. Mild injuries might resolve in 4-8 weeks, while more serious cases can take several months. Our personalized rehabilitation programs are designed to accelerate your recovery through targeted exercises, manual therapy, and modern treatment techniques. During your free consultation, we'll assess your specific situation and provide a realistic recovery timeline. Most importantly, we focus on quality recovery - ensuring you return to full function rather than just temporary relief.

Not having health insurance doesn't prevent you from accessing our services. If you were injured in a car accident, you're likely covered under California's Personal Injury Protection (PIP) insurance, which is separate from your health insurance. Your auto insurance typically includes up to $10,000 in PIP coverage that covers medical treatment related to the accident. We work directly with your auto insurance company to bill them for your rehabilitation services. Additionally, if you receive a settlement from your case, medical liens can be paid from those funds. We'll never turn away someone who needs help due to lack of insurance.

While we maintain relationships with highly qualified personal injury attorneys who specialize in car accident cases, you're never obligated to use our referrals. If you already have an attorney, we'll coordinate seamlessly with them. If you'd like our recommendations, we'll connect you with trusted partners who have proven track records of maximizing settlements. Our goal is to support your recovery and ensure you have the best possible legal representation, whether that comes through our network or elsewhere.

Pre Chiropractic specializes in treating a wide range of injuries commonly resulting from car accidents, including whiplash, neck and back injuries, shoulder injuries, knee injuries, and soft tissue damage. We also handle more complex cases involving multiple injuries or chronic pain conditions. Our comprehensive rehabilitation programs use evidence-based techniques including chiropractic care, physical therapy, massage therapy, and modern therapeutic equipment. Beyond physical injuries, we understand the emotional and financial stress of accidents and provide coordinated support to address your complete wellbeing. If you're unsure whether we can help with your specific injury, please call us for a free consultation.
